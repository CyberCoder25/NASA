Recognition for Boy Scouts and Girl Scouts

NASA recognizes Boy Scouts earning the prestigious Eagle Rank and Girl Scouts earning the prestigious Gold Award. Certificates may be downloaded on the honor system and should only be downloaded after the scout has earned the award. Please be advised that we are no longer producing paper copies of the certificates.
